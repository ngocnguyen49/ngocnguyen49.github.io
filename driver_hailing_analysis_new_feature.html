PROJECT: PRODUCT ANALYSIS

DATA-DRIVEN INSIGHTS & DASHBOARDS

OVERVIEW

This project focuses on conducting a product analysis to uncover actionable insights. It highlights business performance, customer behaviors, and market trends through structured data-driven evaluation.

OBJECTIVES

The main objective is to evaluate product performance across multiple dimensions. The project aims to identify key metrics, trends, and opportunities that drive growth and profitability.

DATASET

The dataset contains sales transactions, customer demographics, and product details. It includes fields such as product category, sales, profit, order date, region, and customer segment, enabling multi-level analysis.

ASSUMPTION

Assumptions made during analysis include:

  • Data is complete and accurately represents business transactions.
  • Seasonality effects are consistent year over year.
  • No major changes occurred in product definitions or categories.

ANALYSIS AND INSIGHTS

Key findings from the analysis:

  • Top-performing products consistently contribute to over 40% of revenue.
  • Customer retention rates are higher in the corporate segment compared to others.
  • Region-specific trends highlight Europe as a strong market for profitability, while Asia shows potential growth.

RECOMMENDATION

Based on the insights, the following recommendations are made:

  • Invest in expanding the product categories that show consistent profit margins.
  • Enhance loyalty programs for consumer and self-employed segments to improve retention.
  • Target emerging markets with customized marketing strategies to capture growth opportunities.
