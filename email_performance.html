PROJECT: EMAIL PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

EMAIL PER DASHBOARD || POWER BI

BACKGROUND INFORMATION

In the ever-evolving digital marketing landscape, email remains one of the most powerful tools for customer communication and engagement. The Email Performance Dashboard, built using Power BI, provides key insights into campaign effectiveness, audience behavior, and delivery performance.

This dashboard serves as a strategic command center, helping marketing teams monitor open rates, click-throughs, bounce rates, and conversions in real-time. It empowers businesses to optimize subject lines, timing, and targeting based on actual user response and engagement patterns.

Designed for decision-makers and marketing professionals, this project sheds light on what drives successful email campaigns. It transforms raw email metrics into clear, actionable insights that improve communication strategies, boost ROI, and foster lasting customer relationships.

OBJECTIVE

The objective is to visualize key metrics and performance indicators that help evaluate the effectiveness of email marketing campaigns. This includes analyzing open rates, click rates, bounce rates, and conversions to uncover trends in audience engagement and message performance. The dashboard enables decision-makers to extract actionable insights that support data-driven optimization of email content, targeting, and timing.

CHAPTER 1: DATA PREPROCESSING

Before creating the dashboards, the raw dataset was cleaned and prepared in Power BI using Power Query. Major preprocessing steps included:

  • Removing duplicates and handling missing values
  • Converting date fields for proper time-based analysis

CHAPTER 2: PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD

The performance dashboard summarizes the email performance with key indicators.

  • The open rate was 24.22%, which is significantly below the 100% target.
  • The highest number of emails sent occurred in September, reaching 36,000.
  • Open rate dropped sharply in August and continued to decline slightly in September.
  • Among campaign types, Promotional emails had the highest open rate.
  • By conversion goal, campaigns targeting form completion led in open rate.

CHAPTER 3: GEOGRAPHICAL DASHBOARD

This dashboard focuses on geographical analysis.

  • Belarus, Iceland, Liberia, and Liechtenstein were the only countries that achieved the 100% open rate target.
  • India had the highest total email volume, sending 36,000 emails, far surpassing other countries.
  • The map visualization highlights coverage across Asia, Africa, Europe, the Americas, and Australia.
  • India, Nigeria, and the United States were the top three countries in terms of device usage, with tablets being the most commonly used device across all three.

CHAPTER 4: CAMPAIGN DASHBOARD

This section explores how different campaigns perform.

  • Newsletter campaigns accounted for the largest share of total emails sent, representing 56.39% of the total — over 52,000 emails.
  • The top-performing campaign names in terms of open rate included:"Event Topic Survey", "Discount Offer for Active Influencers"
  • In terms of volume, the "September Newsletter" and "August Newsletter" campaigns had the highest number of emails sent.
