BACKGROUND INFORMATION

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce performance using Power BI. The dataset includes historical records of sales, profits, customer purchases, and product categories across multiple regions. The purpose of the analysis is to visualize performance trends, uncover patterns in customer behavior, and support strategic decision-making.

OBJECTIVE

The aim of this project is to design a series of interactive dashboards that allow users to monitor sales KPIs, analyze regional performance, evaluate market distribution, and understand product and segment-level behavior over time. By doing so, the dashboard empowers stakeholders to make informed, data-driven business decisions.

CHAPTER 1: DATA PREPROCESSING

The raw dataset was imported into Power BI and cleaned using Power Query. Key steps in data preprocessing included:

  • Converting date formats for accurate time-based analysis
  • Handling missing or null values
  • Creating calculated columns such as Profit Margin, Year, and Month
  • Standardizing category and region names

CHAPTER 2: OVERVIEW DASHBOARD

The overview dashboard serves as the main entry point for business stakeholders. It highlights:

  • Total sales, profit, and quantity sold over time
  • KPI cards showing overall business performance
  • Sales trends visualized via time series charts
  • Top product categories and high-performing customers

CHAPTER 3: MARKET DASHBOARD

This section of the dashboard dives deeper into market-level analysis. Users can:

  • Compare sales and profit by geographical regions
  • Identify the most profitable countries
  • Analyze customer count and average spending by market
  • Filter by time period or product type to narrow insights

CHAPTER 4: SEGMENT AND CATEGORIES DASHBOARD

This final section focuses on customer segments and product categories. Key features include:

  • Performance breakdown by segment: Corporate, Consumer, Home Office
  • Trend analysis for product types like Beauty, Body Care, etc.
  • Profit margin comparison across segments and categories
  • Visual identification of high-volume and high-profit areas
