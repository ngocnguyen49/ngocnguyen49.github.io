PROJECT: E-COMMERCE-BEAUTY ANALYSIS

E-COMMERCE BEAUTY DASHBOARD || POWER BI

BACKGROUND INFORMATION

In the fast-paced e-commerce beauty world, understanding your market and customers is vital. The E-commerce Beauty Analysis Dashboard, powered by Power BI, is your key to unlocking insights into top-selling products, customer engagement, and emerging trends.

This dashboard acts as a strategic compass, helping businesses identify best-sellers, optimize marketing, and predict future trends. It transforms vast sales data into actionable insights.

Serving as a guide for leaders and managers, this project helps you understand what truly drives success in beauty e-commerce. It's about grasping customer desires and behaviors to make smart decisions, building a dynamic market presence where both your brand and customers thrive.

OBJECTIVE

The objective is to visualize key metrics and performance indicators that help uncover trends in sales and profit, understand customer preferences, and evaluate the effectiveness of market and product strategies across segments. The dashboard enables decision-makers to extract actionable insights quickly and clearly.

CHAPTER 1: DATA PREPROCESSING

Before creating the dashboards, the raw dataset was cleaned and prepared in Power BI using Power Query. Major preprocessing steps included:

  • Removing duplicates and handling missing values
  • Converting date fields for proper time-based analysis
  • Creating new columns like Year, Month, Profit Margin
  • Standardizing region and category names

CHAPTER 2: OVERVIEW DASHBOARD

The overview dashboard summarizes the business performance with key indicators.

  • The business maintained a healthy profit margin of 16.35%, indicating solid profitability after accounting for all costs.
  • Notable monthly spikes in sales were observed during June, September, and December, suggesting strong seasonal demand, possibly linked to mid-year promotions and year-end holiday campaigns.
  • Western Europe led all regions in total sales, contributing 27.11% of overall revenue — highlighting it as a key market for the business.
  • Sales have shown consistent annual growth from 2020 through 2023, reflecting effective sales strategies and increasing market presence over time.

CHAPTER 3: MARKET DASHBOARD

This dashboard focuses on market-level analysis.

  • Europe is the most profitable market, contributing 31.13% of total profit, followed by Asia Pacific in second place.
  • Asia Pacific leads in total sales, with Europe ranking second, highlighting strong revenue performance across both regions.
  • The United States has the highest number of customers, reflecting a large and engaged customer base.
  • Additionally, the U.S. is the country with the highest customer spending, indicating strong purchasing power and high-value transactions.

CHAPTER 4: SEGMENT AND CATEGORIES DASHBOARD

This section explores how different segments and product categories perform.

  • Corporate is the top-performing segment, leading in both sales and profit. It is followed by the Consumer segment in second place, and Self-Employed in third.
  • Profit trends show consistent growth across all segments (Corporate, Consumer, and Self-Employed) from 2020 to 2022. However, there was a slight decline in 2023 across all three segments, indicating a potential shift in market dynamics or rising costs.
  • In terms of product categories: Body Care recorded the highest quantity sold, Home & Accessories ranked second, Makeup came in third.
