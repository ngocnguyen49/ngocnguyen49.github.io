BACKGROUND INFORMATION

In the fast-paced e-commerce beauty world, understanding your market and customers is vital. The E-commerce Beauty Analysis Dashboard, powered by Power BI, is your key to unlocking insights into top-selling products, customer engagement, and emerging trends.

This dashboard acts as a strategic compass, helping businesses identify best-sellers, optimize marketing, and predict future trends. It transforms vast sales data into actionable insights.

Serving as a guide for leaders and managers, this project helps you understand what truly drives success in beauty e-commerce. It's about grasping customer desires and behaviors to make smart decisions, building a dynamic market presence where both your brand and customers thrive.

OBJECTIVE

The objective is to visualize key metrics and performance indicators that help uncover trends in sales and profit, understand customer preferences, and evaluate the effectiveness of market and product strategies across segments. The dashboard enables decision-makers to extract actionable insights quickly and clearly.

CHAPTER 1: DATA PREPROCESSING

Before creating the dashboards, the raw dataset was cleaned and prepared in Power BI using Power Query. Major preprocessing steps included:

  • Removing duplicates and handling missing values
  • Converting date fields for proper time-based analysis
  • Creating new columns like Year, Month, Profit Margin
  • Standardizing region and category names

CHAPTER 2: OVERVIEW DASHBOARD

The overview dashboard summarizes the business performance with key indicators such as:

  • Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity Sold
  • Sales and profit trends over time
  • Top-performing categories and customers
  • Dynamic filtering by time period and region

CHAPTER 3: MARKET DASHBOARD

This dashboard focuses on market-level analysis. It highlights:

  • Sales and profit by region and country
  • Customer count and average order value by market
  • Regional performance comparisons
  • Trends that inform marketing and expansion decisions

CHAPTER 4: SEGMENT AND CATEGORIES DASHBOARD

This section explores how different segments and product categories perform. It features:

  • Segment-wise breakdown (Corporate, Consumer, Home Office)
  • Performance of product types such as Beauty, Body Care, etc.
  • Profit margin and volume comparison across segments
  • Visual exploration of which categories drive the most value
